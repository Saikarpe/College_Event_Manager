Our Mission

To impart quality technical education by fostering innovation, ethical values, and research-driven learning — empowering students to become industry-ready engineers and leaders of tomorrow.

🔭
Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of academic excellence, producing technically competent, socially responsible, and entrepreneurially oriented graduates who contribute to the nation's growth.

💡
Core Values

Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity and Impact — the four pillars that guide every academic program, student activity, and institutional initiative at SCE.

Campus Life

Beyond the Classroom

🏆
Competitions & Fests

Annual flagship events like Technosanjivani (technical fest) and Tarang (cultural fest) draw participants from colleges across Maharashtra.

🤝
Industry Connect

Strong ties with 200+ companies for internships, placements, and live project collaborations through a dedicated Training & Placement cell.

🔬
Research & Innovation

State-of-the-art research labs, an Innovation Hub, and dedicated funding for student projects and patents — fostering the next generation of inventors.

⚽
Sports & Wellness

Sprawling sports complex with cricket ground, basketball courts, gymnasium and an active sports committee promoting athlete excellence.
