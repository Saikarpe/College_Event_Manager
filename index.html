About CampusConnect

Learn more about our platform, institution and how to reach us

🎯

Our Mission

CampusConnect bridges the gap between departments and students by providing a unified platform for all campus events. We aim to make event discovery seamless and encourage cross-department collaboration and participation.

🏫

The Institution

Our college is a premier institution offering programs in Computer Science, Information Technology, Electronics, Mechanical, Electrical, Mechatronics, Structural, and Civil Engineering. With a vibrant community of 5,000+ students and 8 departments, innovation thrives here every day.
